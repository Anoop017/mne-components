About Us

MNE Components India Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Nashik, Maharashtra, is a reputed manufacturer of EV battery pack components, including cell-to-cell interconnects, busbars, and sub-assemblies.

We provide end-to-end engineered solutions in Copper, Aluminum, Clad Metals, and Stainless Steel to serve automotive OEMs, Energy Storage Systems, and switchgear manufacturers.

We are committed to delivering innovation, reliability, and consistent quality from initial prototypes to full-scale production.

Our Products

Cell-to-Cell Connectors

  • Compatible with cylindrical, prismatic, and pouch cell types
  • Made from Copper, Aluminium & Clad Materials
  • Designed for EV battery systems and ESS modules

Flexible & Hybrid Busbars

  • Laminated busbars in Copper and Aluminum
  • High current carrying capacity with excellent vibration performance
  • Customizable for layout, insulation, and thermal needs

Stamped & Formed Components

  • Manufactured using high-speed progressive stamping
  • Materials: Cu, Ni, Al, Clad & Stainless Steel
  • Precision-engineered for high-volume production

Laser Welded Components

  • Suitable for both similar and dissimilar metal joining
  • Clean, strong, and reliable connections
  • Ideal for high-power battery pack applications
